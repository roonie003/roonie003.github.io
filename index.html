Our Story

Freedom Café was originally cooked up as a small, relaxed restaurant adjoining The Concierge Boutique Bungalows. Over the years it has become the heart and soul of the hotel. Brilliant for breakfasts and lazy lunches. Explore Freedom Cafe gallery free your mind, body and soul at Freedom Cafe.

Great Location

Located in Greyville, Freedom Cafe is set in the heart of Durban and home to a magnificent wild fig tree that is a part of the ambience. It is a perfect spot for working, meetings or simply relax.

Events

Freedom Cafe has the most initmate setting which makes it perfect for hosting amazing occassions. It is popular for high-tea, baby-showers, birthdays or just a great family get together.
